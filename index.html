Municipal Engineering Office

Dedicated to providing efficient public service and ensuring compliance with municipal ordinances for construction and development projects in Baras, Rizal.

About Us

The Municipal Engineering Office of Baras, Rizal, headed by Engr. Donna Paz M. Fullante, is responsible for the planning, implementation, and supervision of all municipal infrastructure projects. The office ensures compliance with engineering standards and national building regulations in support of the municipality’s development goals.
